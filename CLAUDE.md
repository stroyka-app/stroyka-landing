# Stroyka Landing

Marketing and client-facing website for the Stroyka construction job-costing app.

- **Product**: Stroyka — a construction job-costing application (Flutter/Dart, Supabase backend)
- **This repo**: Marketing/landing site to promote and sell the Stroyka app
- **Related repo**: `job-costing-app` — the actual Stroyka product (separate repo)
- **Related repo**: `promstroy-website` — family construction business website (separate concern)

WOW Interaction Effects

Pick the effects you want on the Stroyka landing page. You can select multiple.

A

Falling Letter Effect

A letter from "STROYKA" in the hero detaches and tumbles down with physics as you scroll, rolling back up when you scroll back. Inspired by side-kick.se. The letter could be the "K" or "Y" — whichever looks most dramatic falling from the word.

B

Cursor Dot Follower

A smooth, trailing dot that follows the mouse cursor across the page. Subtle sage-green glow. Grows slightly when hovering interactive elements. Like the Promstroy website effect you loved.

C

Hero 3D/Video Element

Instead of a static screenshot in the hero, use a slowly rotating 3D-style isometric construction scene or a looping ambient video (dark, moody jobsite aerial). Creates depth and premium feel immediately.

D

Magnetic CTA Buttons

CTA buttons subtly pull toward the cursor when it's nearby (within ~100px), then snap back when the cursor leaves. Feels alive and tactile. Common on premium agency sites like the ones you referenced.

E

Scroll-Driven Text Reveal

Section headlines reveal word-by-word or letter-by-letter as you scroll into them — each word/letter fading and sliding up in sequence. More dramatic than simple fade-up. Think editorial/magazine feel.

F

Parallax Feature Cards

Feature cards move at different speeds on scroll, creating a layered depth effect. Cards tilt slightly on hover with a 3D perspective transform. Shadows shift dynamically based on cursor position.

G

Lenis Smooth Scroll

Replace native scroll with Lenis for buttery-smooth, inertia-based scrolling. This is the foundation that makes ALL other scroll effects feel premium. Used by side-kick.se and paysages.studio.

H

Section Color Transitions

Background color subtly shifts between sections as you scroll — from midnight to slightly lighter deep teal and back. Creates a sense of progression through the page without harsh section breaks.

Select all the ones you want. I recommend A + B + D + E + G at minimum for the WOW factor you're after.

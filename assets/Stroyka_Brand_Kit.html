05 — Logo Usage
Website Navbar
Full combination mark (Cornerstone + STROYKA), dark variant. Height: 32–36px. On brand-midnight background.
Website Footer
Full combination mark, dark variant. Slightly smaller: 28px height. Same midnight background.
Browser Favicon
Strata mark on brand-midnight square background. Export as PNG: 16×16, 32×32, 64×64. Use 32px for favicon.ico.
iOS / Android App Icon
Strata mark centered on brand-midnight square. Export at 512×512 PNG. flutter_launcher_icons handles all sizes.
Social Media Avatar
Strata mark on brand-midnight circle (or square with rounded corners). 400×400px minimum.
OG / Share Image
1200×630px. Brand-midnight background. Cornerstone + STROYKA left-aligned. Tagline in brand-sage. Keep it minimal.
✓ Do
Use the dark variant on dark/midnight backgrounds. Use the light variant on white/light backgrounds. Maintain clear space equal to the mark height on all sides. Scale proportionally.
✗ Don't
Don't place the dark variant on white (low contrast). Don't stretch or distort the mark. Don't change the bracket colors or angles. Don't add drop shadows or effects to the mark. Don't place on busy photographic backgrounds.

07 — Next Steps
01
Export PNG favicons — Open favicon-strata-dark.svg in a browser, then use a tool like svgtopng.com or Figma to export at 16, 32, 64, and 180px. Place in stroyka-landing/public/.
02
Export app icon PNG — Open app-icon-512.svg in a browser, export as 512×512 PNG. Add to the Flutter project's assets and configure flutter_launcher_icons in pubspec.yaml.
03
Create OG image — 1200×630px. Background: #2f3e46. Place the Cornerstone mark left-of-center + STROYKA wordmark. Add tagline "Construction Management for Real Crews" in #84a98c below. Export as PNG → public/og-image.png.
04
Use Logo.tsx component — The landing page prompt includes a complete Logo.tsx React component that renders the Cornerstone mark as inline SVG. No image files needed for the web logo — it's all code.
05
Store originals in Google Drive — Upload the entire brand/ folder to your Google Drive under Stroyka / Brand & Marketing / Brand Identity. These SVGs are your master files.
